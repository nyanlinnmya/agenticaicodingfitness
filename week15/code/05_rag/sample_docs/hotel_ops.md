# Smart Hotel Operations Handbook (excerpt)

## Comfort setpoints
The lobby should be kept at 24°C during the day and may drift to 26°C overnight
to save energy. Guest rooms target 23°C when occupied. Function halls are
pre-cooled to 22°C one hour before a booked event.

## Energy program
The building's baseline consumption is about 10,000 kWh per day. The energy
optimization program targets 7,000 kWh per day — a 30% reduction — without
dropping guest comfort below the agreed thresholds.

## HVAC overrides
If a room records more than three manual temperature overrides in a single
month, it is flagged for predictive maintenance: the thermostat or sensor may
be miscalibrated.
