Energy & HVAC Incident Report — Room 305

On 2026-05-20 at 09:12, the air conditioner AC-305 (Mitsubishi inverter unit) in
Room 305 raised an HVAC fault alert (ALT-305) of MEDIUM severity. The unit
reported an inverter board error and fell back to fixed-speed operation, raising
energy use sharply.

SensorReadings for Room 305 showed humidity holding at 68% and energy draw of
2.1 kWh/h versus a 1.2 kWh/h baseline. Guest G-301 (checked in 2026-05-19,
checked out 2026-05-22) reported discomfort to the front desk.

Technician Somchai (Staff S-12) was assigned to ALT-305 and performed
MaintenanceJob MJ-77 (type=HVAC), replacing the inverter board. MJ-77 RESOLVES
ALT-305 and is FOR_ROOM 305. The job completed the same day. AC-305 is provided
by CoolAir Co. (Thailand). Post-repair energy use returned to the 1.2 kWh/h
baseline, confirming the fix.
