💻 vs 🖥️ — where do I run each command?

💻 Your laptop runs this tutorial (the web app you're looking at). 🖥️ The DGX runs the real models. The laptop talks to the DGX over the connection shown in the pills (top-right).

The golden rule

  • 💻 The … tutorial_server.py command (how you launched this) and any export DGX_… / BRAIN=… run on your laptop.
  • 🖥️ Every grey shell block inside a chapter (docker, ollama pull, vllm, nemo, litellm, ngrok…) runs on the DGX. The app only shows them — it never runs them on your laptop.

Connect this app to your DGX (set on the 💻 laptop, then reload)

# brain: who thinks —  BRAIN=local (DGX) | claude (cloud) | sim (offline)
# DGX_CONN: how you reach the local model —
export DGX_CONN=local                          # ① DGX on your LAN / this laptop

export DGX_CONN=tunnel                          # ② DGX over an ngrok tunnel
export DGX_TUNNEL_URL="https://user:pass@<id>.ngrok-free.app/v1"
#   🖥️ on the DGX:  ngrok http 11434 --host-header="localhost:11434" --basic-auth="user:pass"

export DGX_CONN=cloud                           # ③ cloud Ollama
export DGX_CLOUD_URL=https://ollama.com/v1
export DGX_API_KEY=<key>
