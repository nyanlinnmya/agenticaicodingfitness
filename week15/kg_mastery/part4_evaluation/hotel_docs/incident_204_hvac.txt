HVAC Incident Report — Room 204

On 2026-05-18 at 14:30, device AC-204 (Daikin split unit, installed 2023-04-01)
in Room 204 triggered a HIGH severity temperature alert (ALT-204). The room
temperature climbed to 29.4C while the setpoint was 23C, indicating the
compressor was not engaging.

Technician Somchai (Staff S-12, Maintenance shift) was assigned to alert
ALT-204 and performed MaintenanceJob MJ-66 (type=HVAC). The root cause was a
clogged condensate drain causing a safety cutout. The job started at 15:10 and
completed at 16:05; MJ-66 RESOLVES ALT-204.

Energy impact: Room 204 consumed 38% more kWh than its 7-day baseline during the
fault window because the unit short-cycled. After repair, consumption returned to
baseline. The supplier of AC-204 is CoolAir Co. (Thailand, category=HVAC).
